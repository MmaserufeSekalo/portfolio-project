Mmaserufe Sekalo, is young creative born and raised in the northern province, Limpopo, South Africa. Mmaserufe graduated with a degree in agriculture with miltiple interests including, but not limited to photography and content creation. Since graduation, Mmaserufe enjoyed living on the road for work, which involved mostly local travel and international travel until December 2022.

Outside work, Mmaserufe enjoys meeting new people, running and playing games

Some of Mmaserufe's achievement include recent nomination for World Food Programme Summit 2022 as Africa's Brightest Young Mind intended formulate solutions to Feed the Future, in partnership with BYM.
